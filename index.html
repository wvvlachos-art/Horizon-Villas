The location

Close to everything, quietly

Town, without the bustle

Tinos Town and the port are fifteen minutes by car — close enough for the market and the evening volta, far enough that the nights stay yours.

Three beaches, minutes away

Agios Ioannis below the houses, Agios Sostis next door, Pachia Ammos for wilder sand — a different mood at each.

The cape itself

A green headland of tamarisks, dunes and quiet coves — footpaths along the water before the rest of the bay is awake.

Sheltered from the meltemi

When the summer wind blows hard across the island, this south-facing corner stays calm — beach days hold up even in August.
