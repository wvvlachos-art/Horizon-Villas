The place

The quiet side of the Cyclades

Horizon is a small collection of six modern houses, gathered by our family around one shared pool on a quiet hillside above Agios Ioannis. Five minutes from the beach, a world away from the crowds — the Cyclades at its most unhurried.

The island

The Cyclades, quietly

Marble villages and dovecotes, a serious food scene, footpaths over the hills, and beaches you can still have to yourself. Tinos rewards the traveller who skips Mykonos — never more so than in spring and autumn.

Explore Tinos

The local area

Five minutes from the good stuff

Agios Ioannis keeps everything close — the water, a few good tavernas, and the rest of the island just up the road.

Agios Ioannis beach

A five-minute walk down to a quiet swimming cove and a beach taverna.

Eat & shop

Family tavernas, a bakery and a small market within the village.

Tinos Town & port

The harbour, ferries and the main shops, about fifteen minutes by car.

Villages & trails
