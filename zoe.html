The house

A bright loft for two, the Aegean filling its windows. The bedroom sits on the ground floor with a king bed and a Jacuzzi bathtub; upstairs, an open living room with dining, a grey-linen sofa, and a pergola-shaded terrace with daybeds. A balcony over the sea, a veranda with a garden.
