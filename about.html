About

A hillside, a pool, six houses

A small collection of six modern houses sharing one pool, above Agios Ioannis — the quiet side of the Cyclades.

Horizon is a small collection of six modern houses in a row on the hillside above Agios Ioannis, sharing one sea-view pool — the quiet side of the Cyclades.

Horizon began the way these things often do on a Greek island — slowly, one house at a time.

It sits on a quiet hillside above Agios Ioannis. Over the years six houses were built in a row along the hillside, with the pool at one end — the same honest design throughout, framed by the same view of the Aegean. They're named Zoe, Diana, Regina, Celine, Roxana and Astra.

Premium, not luxury

We're not a resort, and we don't pretend to be. No spa, no restaurant, no private pools — just six comfortable houses, one shared pool, and five minutes between you and the beach.

Premium, the way we see it, is getting the basics right and being honest about the rest: daily cleaning, fresh linens, and a quick reply on WhatsApp when you need one.

We'd rather promise a little less and surprise you, than the other way around.

— William Vlachos, your host

More information

Good to know

How many houses are there?

Six — Zoe, Diana, Regina, Celine, Roxana and Astra — all in a row on one hillside, sharing a single pool.

Do all the houses share the pool?

Yes. One pool, shared by all six — it sits at one end of the row of houses, under a minute from any door. It's the heart of the place.

What's included in a stay?

Daily cleaning, fresh linens, fast wifi and free parking — in every house, every stay.

Is it suitable for families?

Very. Several houses sleep up to six, the pool is the centre of the day, and the beach is a five-minute walk down.

How do we get there?

Fly to Athens or Mykonos, then take the ferry to Tinos. We're about fifteen minutes from the port, above Agios Ioannis — and we'll help you plan the journey.

When is the best time to visit?

Spring (April to early June) and autumn (mid-September to October) are our favourites — warm, quiet, and the island at its best.

How far is the beach?

About five minutes on foot — a quiet swimming cove below the village, with a beach taverna for lunch.

Is there a minimum stay?

Usually a few nights, and it varies by season. Tell us your dates and we'll let you know what works.

Can we book directly?

Always — and it's the best price. Book direct and you deal with us, with no commission in between.
