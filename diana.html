The house

A two-level house with a deep-blue thread running through it. Downstairs, an open-plan living room with a navy sofa, a dining table and a full kitchen; up its own staircase, two double bedrooms — one with a sun-yellow headboard — and a polished-concrete bathroom. The pergola-shaded terrace looks clear across the bay.
